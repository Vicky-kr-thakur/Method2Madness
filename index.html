Enroll to embrace your madness of innovation and creativity

Method2Madness is a unique first of a kind Experience enabled skill development platform where exceptional talents of the field come together and cultivate the skillset to sustain the present industrial demands. Our unique filtering process of only selecting top startups which are pioneer in their fields are either profitable or funded by relevant VCs. We being not an edtech startup are an Edex startup i.e. an educational startup fueled by experience and excellence of exceptional founders and mentors who proved themselves early in life so that your mentor is not a generation away. We at MTMD are trying to bridge the campus to corporate gap so that a student sitting in his dorm room knows what is expected out of him so as to command relevancy and employment once he/she graduates and not being counted amongst the 97% of his/her peers deemed unskilled.
